SOMETHING IS BEING ARRANGED
· · ·
Let your hotel lobby emerge its mental doors
with my beach house.

Some rooms are not found, but returned to —
the ones we thought we'd memorized,
until a door we'd passed a hundred times
opens onto someone new.

At night, it's coming.
The question was never how well you know someone.
It was whether you're still willing to find out.
DO NOT DISTURB"...until the hour turns, and the lobby empties, and someone finally arrives."
PATIENCE IS PART OF THE EVENING
